# Hjemla lawyers (Advokatene)

Is something not right with your home? The Hjemla lawyers specialise in property law and defect (reklamasjon) cases, and can assist broadly within real estate — in partnership with Advokatfirmaet Solver.

## What they help with

- Defects discovered after purchase (complaints / reklamasjon).
- An independent "second opinion" when an insurer rejects a claim.
- Broad assistance within real estate for buying and selling.

## How to get in touch

- Submit your case free of charge via `advokat.hjemla.no/registrer`.
- Call 22 44 10 00 (weekdays 09:00–15:00).

:::tip Free choice of lawyer
If you have home-buyer or contents insurance, you decide who handles your case.
:::
