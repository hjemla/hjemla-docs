# Who it's for

Hjemla is built for ordinary people at every stage of the housing journey — not only for professionals.

## Buyers

Explore the map, see what's for sale and what recently sold nearby. Use the Hjemla estimate and sales history to judge whether a home is fairly priced before you bid.

## Sellers

Get a picture of price levels and activity in the neighbourhood, see what to expect on price, and test interest with no obligation using "Considered for sale" before you decide.

## Home owners

Stay updated on your own home's value over time, follow your neighbours' sale prices, and keep your property information correct in "My home".

## Curious neighbours

Many people simply use Hjemla to follow the market and see what homes in the area are worth or were sold for.
