# About Hjemla

Hjemla is a digital housing platform where you can see everything about every home, district and neighbourhood in Norway — for free.

A home is the biggest investment most people make, yet the decision is often taken under time pressure and with incomplete information. Hjemla gathers a home's value, characteristics and market context in one place so that owners, buyers and sellers can make safer choices. The goal is a more honest, safer and more understandable housing market.

Hjemla gives private individuals access to much of the same information that professionals — agents, surveyors, banks and investors — have always had.

## What you'll find here

- **[Property map](../kjernefunksjoner/boligkart.md)** — a nationwide map of homes for sale, recently sold, and being considered for sale.
- **[The Hjemla estimate](../kjernefunksjoner/hjemlaestimatet.md)** — an automated value estimate for every private home in Norway.
- **[My home](../kjernefunksjoner/min-bolig.md)** — claim and manage your own home, track its value and document work.
- **[Neighbourhoods](../kjernefunksjoner/nabolag-og-boligmarked.md)** — prices, trends and insight for cities and districts.
