# Considered for sale (Vurderes solgt)

With "Considered for sale", Hjemla highlights your home on the map and in front of thousands of visitors, without committing you to a sale. Completely non-binding, completely free.

## Benefits

- **Test the market** — get an indication of interest in your specific home.
- **Gather interest** — attract serious buyers before you potentially sell.
- **Safe and non-binding** — you don't have to decide on a sale yet.

## How it works

1. **Set as considered for sale** — go to "My home" and follow the steps. You set the asking price yourself.
2. **Dedicated page** — the home gets its own page aimed at potential buyers.
3. **Follow along** — you get a dashboard with interesting data about the interest.

:::tip
You turn the feature on from [My home](./min-bolig.md).
:::
