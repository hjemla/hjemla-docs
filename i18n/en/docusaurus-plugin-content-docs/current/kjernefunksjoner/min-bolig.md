# My home (Min bolig)

My home is your own logged-in workspace. Claim your address and unlock a private dashboard for the home.

## What you can do

- **Track the value** — see the home's estimated market value and how it changes over time.
- **Edit home data** — correct facilities, attributes and documentation. The estimate recalculates.
- **Follow the neighbourhood** — see your neighbours' sale prices and what's happening in your area.
- **Considered for sale** — flag the home as possibly for sale to test interest.

## Getting started

1. Log in to Hjemla.
2. Search for your address and choose "My home".
3. Confirm that you own the home and complete the claim.
4. Update the information for a more precise estimate.

:::note
This feature requires an account. See [Account & settings](../konto-og-hjelp/konto-innstillinger.md).
:::
